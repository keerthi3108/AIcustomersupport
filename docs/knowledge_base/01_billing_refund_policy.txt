SupportAI Billing - Refund Policy
Customers may request a full refund within 14 days of purchase for annual plans and within 7 days for monthly plans. Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5-10 business days. Partial refunds are not available after the trial period ends. To request a refund, submit a billing ticket with your invoice number and reason. Enterprise contracts follow custom SLA terms defined in the signed agreement.
